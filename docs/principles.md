# Principles

---

## Core Principles

### 1. Separation of Cognition and Execution

Decision-making must be independent from execution authority.

---

### 2. Sovereign Identity

Agents must be uniquely identifiable and accountable.

---

### 3. Bounded Autonomy

Autonomy must operate within defined limits.

---

### 4. Auditability

All actions must be traceable and verifiable.

---

### 5. Trust Minimization

Systems should not rely on implicit trust.

---

### 6. Resistance to Capture

No entity should gain unilateral control over execution.

---

### 7. Technological Adaptability

The framework must remain valid across evolving technologies.

---
