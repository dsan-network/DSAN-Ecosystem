# Governance

---

## Evolution Model

The DSAN Ecosystem follows an evolutionary governance model:

1. Initial coordination
2. Hybrid governance
3. Distributed governance

---

## Proposal System

Changes are introduced via structured proposals including:

* Problem definition
* Proposed change
* Impact analysis
* Risk assessment

---

## Constraints

No proposal may violate core principles.

---

## Objective

Ensure long-term sustainability and resistance to centralization.
